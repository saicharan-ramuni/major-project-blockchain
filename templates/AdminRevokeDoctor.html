Global Doctor Revocation

Permanently revoke a doctor's access across ALL patients. This is the Admin-layer revocation in the KAC-UR dual-layer scheme.

Admin-Layer Global Revocation (KAC-UR Layer 2)

This implements the Admin Revocation layer from Liu et al., IEEE TKDE 2024. Once a doctor is globally revoked, the KAC-UR engine will deny Update Key generation for that doctor across every patient in the system — regardless of whether individual patients have revoked them or not. This is appropriate for emergency situations such as license revocation or malicious behavior detection.

⚠ Danger Zone — Irreversible Action

Global revocation is recorded permanently on the blockchain and invalidates the doctor's Update Keys across all patients. The in-memory KAC engine state is also immediately updated. This action cannot be undone without re-deploying the contract and re-running the migration.
